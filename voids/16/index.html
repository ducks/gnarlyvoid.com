A wavy, blue, framebreaking tufted fiber piece

March 18, 2026

#fiber #tufting

A study in movement and restraint, this tufted piece uses a sequence of flowing, offset waves to create depth without relying on hard contrast. The palette moves from deep cobalt through pale mint into soft seafoam and gray, evoking water, erosion, and layered natural forms. Its irregular silhouette breaks away from the traditional rectangular rug, allowing the composition to expand outward and feel uncontained. The result is a tactile, sculptural surface that shifts with light and perspective - somewhere between landscape and motion captured in fiber.
