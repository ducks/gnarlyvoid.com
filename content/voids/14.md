This hand-tufted rug explores organic movement through layered green tones
and sculpted pile. Deep forest green "drips" cascade over lighter mint and
cream shapes, creating a fluid, melting effect that feels both natural and
surreal. The carved texture enhances the dimensional look, giving the piece
a soft topographic quality where colors appear to flow into one another.

Multiple shades of green create depth and contrast, while the high-low
tufting and hand carving emphasize the dripping forms. The result is a
sculptural textile that blurs the line between rug and wall art.

Inspired by liquid motion, mossy landscapes, and experimental fiber design,
this piece works equally well as a small statement rug or as textured fiber
art for a wall.
