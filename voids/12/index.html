Traditional tattoo inspired flower fiber piece

February 18, 2026

#fiber #tufting #flash

A hand-tufted rug inspired by traditional tattoo flash.

This piece features a bold five-petal flower with a bright yellow center, saturated red petals, and heavy black outlines. The white halo gives the design breathing room, while the deep green leaves ground it with contrast and balance.

Built with thick pile and sharp color blocking, it's designed to read clearly from a distance and feel plush up close. The sculpted silhouette follows the leaves and petals, turning the graphic into a functional object.

Classic flash energy, translated into fiber.
